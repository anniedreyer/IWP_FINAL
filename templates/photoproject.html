This summer, I went on a roadtrip
with my friends to explore parts of
Midwest and Southwest America.

Taken throughout the summer of 2018, featured photos include shots in Kansas City Kansas and Missouri, Colorado, Texas, and New Mexico.

Setting aside a week for a roadtrip, my friends and I drove to Amarillo, White Sands, Santa Fe, and Colorado Springs. It was a summer week spent listening to all of the old jams, eating way too much fried food, not enough photos or caffeine.
